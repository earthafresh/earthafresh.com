Our Story

From Indian street corners to the Pacific Coast.

Eartha Fresh was born from a simple memory: the fizz, spice, and joy of masala soda shared with friends and family on warm evenings in India. We wanted to bottle that feeling — and bring it to British Columbia.

A tradition we grew up with.

In India, masala soda isn’t just a drink. It’s a ritual. Fresh lime squeezed into a chilled glass, a pinch of roasted spices, a touch of salt, and a burst of bubbles — mixed on the spot while everyone waits for the first sip.

Those flavors inspired Eartha Fresh. Our Masala Soda takes that classic street-side experience and refines it into a modern, ready-to-enjoy beverage.

Why masala soda feels good.

Traditional masala sodas are often enjoyed after meals and on hot days. They typically combine:

  • Citrus for brightness and refreshment.
  • Warm spices like cumin, black salt, and pepper for depth.
  • A lightly sparkling base that leaves you feeling uplifted.

Eartha Fresh draws inspiration from these time-honoured recipes, using carefully selected ingredients to create a drink that feels comforting, familiar, and refreshingly new.

About Us

Locally made, globally inspired.

Eartha Fresh is a British Columbia–based food and beverage company that blends local craftsmanship with flavors inspired by the world.

Locally crafted in BC.

Every batch of Eartha Fresh Masala Soda is crafted close to home. We work with local partners wherever possible and pay attention to the finer details — from carbonation levels to the feel of the can in your hand.

Being local means we can stay small, flexible, and focused on quality rather than quantity.

Ingredients sourced from around the world.

To capture the soul of masala soda, we look beyond borders. Our recipes are inspired by spices and ingredients traditionally used in Indian kitchens — combined thoughtfully with high-quality components available here in Canada.

The result is a drink that feels familiar to those who grew up with masala soda and excitingly new to those discovering it for the first time.
