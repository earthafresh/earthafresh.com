Brand Story

Minimalist design, maximal flavour.

Eartha Fresh is a British Columbia–based food and beverage brand marrying nostalgic flavours with clean, contemporary design. No clutter—on the palate or on the label.

Why Eartha Fresh?

We started with a simple idea: take the flavours we grew up with and present them in a way that feels at home on the most modern shelves in Vancouver, Toronto or London.

  • Premium look: inspired by the subtle shimmer of a peacock feather, translated into clean lines and gradients.
  • Thoughtful flavour design: balanced spice, not overpowering sweetness.
  • Built for hospitality: perfect for pairing with food, cocktails or tasting menus.
